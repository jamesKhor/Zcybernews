## Executive Summary

ESET's WeLiveSecurity blog argues that cybersecurity provides an underappreciated competitive advantage: the ability to continue operating safely in an environment where competitors cannot. The post frames security investment as a business enabler rather than a cost center, a perspective rarely quantified in boardroom discussions. No specific incidents, vulnerabilities, or threat actors are cited.

## Technical Analysis

The analysis is conceptual rather than technical. It posits that organizations with mature security postures can maintain operations during widespread attacks — ransomware outbreaks, supply chain compromises, or infrastructure disruptions — while less-prepared competitors face downtime, data loss, or regulatory penalties. The post does not provide empirical data, case studies, or specific metrics to support this claim. It is a strategic opinion piece, not a technical report.

## Mitigations & Recommendations

Defenders should consider documenting business continuity and security resilience as measurable operational metrics — such as mean time to detect (MTTD), mean time to respond (MTTR), and uptime during incidents — to communicate security's value to executive leadership. The source material offers no specific technical mitigations.
